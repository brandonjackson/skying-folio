Skying is a creative studio and consulting practice. We work at the intersection of technology, art, and public policy — helping people see the world differently, whether that means planning for what's coming or learning to look up.

Founded by Brandon Jackson, with a background spanning product leadership, AI policy, digital public infrastructure, and a lifelong obsession with painting.

What we do

Scenarios, Policy & Research

Scenario planning workshops, strategic foresight, and policy development for leaders navigating the economic implications of transformative AI.

Product & Technology

Product strategy, technical architecture, and hands-on leadership for organisations building digital public goods and platforms.

Walking Tours

Guided walks through London exploring the history of art, technology, and ideas — from Constable's Hampstead skies to the streets that shaped the industrial age.

Art Commissions

Original paintings on commission. Landscapes, skyscapes, and portraits in oil — working in the tradition of observation and natural light.
